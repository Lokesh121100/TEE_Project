# 📄 Integrated IT End-User Support: Full Tender Specifications

This document captures the verbatim requirements from Section 4.1 (TEE) and 4.2 (Deskside Support) of the tender.

---

## 4.1.9 Mandatory Technical Evaluation Exercise (TEE)

### a. Timeline
- **Invitation**: 2 weeks after tender closure.
- **Preparation**: ~2 weeks.

### b. Evaluation Focus
1. **Portfolio Quality & Use Case Design**: Depth and complexity of the 10 incident scenarios.
2. **Functional Strategic Approach**: Methodology for deploying AI agents and transitioning from human-in-the-loop to full automation.
3. **Live Exercise**: Presentation on implementation approach, demonstration of use cases, and handling ad-hoc scenarios.

## 4.2 Deskside Support Services

### 4.2.1 Onsite Deskside Engineer Support
- **Scope**: L1 and L2 technical support by onsite Desktop Engineers.
- **Corporate IT Device Support**:
    - **Software**: OS errors, M365, VDI, VPN, AV. Prioritize escalation from AI agents.
    - **Hardware**: Initial triage, coordination with OEMs for warranty/repair. Handle handovers via Tech Bars/Smart Lockers.
    - **Integration**: Must complement AI-driven troubleshooting (DEX) and document all activities in ServiceNow.
    - **User Training**: Provide brief education during onsite visits to prevent recurring issues.
    - **Asset Accountability**: Update ServiceNow records (asset tagging, swaps).

### 4.2.1(c) Scientific Work Environments (REs)
- **Physical SIDs**: Transitional support for research devices (OS, network, HW repairs).
- **"One Device" Initiative**: Progressive replacement of SID/CID with a single unified device (from Jun 2026).
- **VDI Support**: Troubleshooting VDI client, connectivity, and scientific peripherals (instruments) within virtual sessions.

### 4.2.1(d) Collaboration & Staffing (REs)
- **Dedicated Support**: Mandatory onsite staffing for Research Entities (1.0 FTE or roaming model).
- **Transition**: L2 support for SIDs will transition to the Contractor.

### 4.2.1(g) Tech Bar Operations
- **Management**: Walk-ins, appointments, device triage.
- **Showcase**: Manage "Pre-Procurement" (staff testing upcoming laptops) and "Post-Procurement" (experience centers).

### 4.2.1(i) Smart Locker Utilization
- **Workflows**:
    - Collection of new/replacement devices.
    - Return of devices (offboarding, repairs).
    - Issuance and return of loaner devices.
    - Distribution of IT peripherals (keyboards, mice, cables).
- **Operational Procedures**:
    - Prompt response to ServiceNow task assignments.
    - Updating task status in real-time upon collection/deposit.
    - Escalation for access issues.
