# 📜 Official TEE Demo Preparation Guide

This is a verbatim copy of the requirements for the Mandatory Technical Evaluation Exercise (TEE).

## 1. AI Virtual Agent – Live Demonstration
- Natural language understanding (NLU)
- RAG-based knowledge retrieval
- Automatic ticket creation in ServiceNow
- Auto categorization and routing (>=90% accuracy target)
- Confidence scoring and routing logic
- Seamless human handover with transcript retention
- **Prepare at least 10 demo scenarios**: Password Reset, VPN Issue, Laptop Slow, Outlook Error, VDI Failure, Software Installation, Printer Issue, WiFi Issue, Device Replacement, New Joiner Request.

## 2. AI Accuracy & Engineering Controls
- Confusion matrix reporting dashboard
- Monthly retraining methodology
- Accuracy improvement tracking
- Model drift monitoring
- Human-in-the-loop governance

## 3. Fulfiller-Facing AI Automation
- VPN automated troubleshooting workflow
- SLA breach prediction and auto-escalation
- Root cause pattern detection
- Case summarization AI

## 4. DEX + AI Integration
- Device health dashboard
- Auto-remediation workflows (restart services, cache clearing)
- Proactive tech refresh recommendations
- Quarterly analytics reporting

## 5. Smart Locker Workflow Demo
- Ticket-triggered locker task generation
- Device deposit & collection tracking
- Status updates in ServiceNow
- Escalation handling for locker issues

## 6. Workforce Transformation Roadmap
- Year 1: 20–30% ticket deflection
- Year 2: 30–40% automation rate
- Year 3: 50–60% auto-resolution
- Staffing evolution & reskilling plan

## 7. Governance & Responsible AI Framework
- Bias mitigation strategy
- Hallucination controls
- Audit & explainability logs
- AI transaction cost governance dashboard

## 8. Recommended Demo Structure (45–60 Minutes)
- **Part 1 – Strategy Overview (10 mins)**: Why this approach, alignment with tender, strategic vision.
- **Part 2 – Live AI Demo (25 mins)**: Focus on Virtual Agent and Fulfiller Automation.
- **Part 3 – DEX & Proactive Support (10 mins)**: Device health and proactive remediation.
- **Part 4 – Governance & Cost Control (10 mins)**: Ethics, privacy, and financial oversight.
