# 📊 Workforce Transformation Roadmap
**For ARIA v2.0 - 3-Year AI-Enabled Service Desk Evolution**

**Status**: CRITICAL BUSINESS DOCUMENT | **Priority**: 🔴 HIGH | **Estimated Time**: 2-3 hours

**Date Created**: March 5, 2026

**Purpose**: Demonstrate to evaluators that ARIA is a responsible AI implementation with clear workforce planning, staff redeployment, and career growth pathways

---

## 📋 Overview

This roadmap shows how ARIA transforms the IT Service Desk over 3 years:
- ✅ Reducing repetitive work through automation
- ✅ Redeploying staff to higher-value strategic work
- ✅ Building new skills and career pathways
- ✅ Managing change with zero involuntary layoffs
- ✅ Increasing job satisfaction and retention

**Expected Outcome**: Evaluators see ARIA as a **workforce enabler**, not a threat to jobs.

## 🎯 YEAR 1: Foundation & Quick Wins (Q2 2026 - Q1 2027)

### Year 1 Targets
- **Ticket Deflection**: 20-30% (self-service + AI)
- **Automation Rate**: 15-20% (auto-resolution)
- **Staff Redeployment**: 10-15% (1-2 FTE)
- **Training Hours**: 40 hours/person (ARIA + new skills)

```

4. Outlook Email Sync Problems         → 80% automated (8% of tickets)
```

## 📈 Change Management Strategy

### Key Principles
1. **No Involuntary Layoffs** - 100% guarantee
2. **Career Growth** - 50% of staff promoted to strategic roles by Year 3
3. **Continuous Learning** - 40 hours/year training for all staff
4. **Fair Compensation** - Salary increases tied to skill development
5. **Transparent Communication** - Monthly town halls explaining ARIA impact

### Change Management Timeline

**Phase 1: Announcement & Planning** (Weeks 1-4)
```
├─ Executive announcement of ARIA initiative
├─ All-hands meeting explaining benefits
├─ Individual career conversations with staff
├─ Skills assessment & development plans
└─ FAQ and concerns handling
```

**Phase 2: Pilot & Training** (Weeks 5-12)
```
├─ ARIA system goes live for password resets
├─ Pilot group (3-4 agents) handles escalations
├─ Training rollout for all staff (40 hours)
├─ Weekly feedback sessions
└─ Real-time adjustments based on feedback
```

**Phase 3: Full Rollout** (Weeks 13-26)
```
├─ ARIA handles 10 automated categories
├─ New "AI Operations" role starts
├─ First career transition: 1 FTE to AI Ops
├─ Monthly metrics review (deflection, automation, satisfaction)
└─ Year 1 assessment & Year 2 planning
```

**Phase 4: Optimization & Growth** (Months 7-12)
```
├─ Strategic support roles created
├─ Career pathways published for all staff
├─ Second wave transitions (2-3 FTE)
├─ Advanced training for new specialties
└─ Year 2 expansion planning
```

### Staff Communication Plan
